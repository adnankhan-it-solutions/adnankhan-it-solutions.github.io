Background

My journey is driven by a deep curiosity about how systems work — technical, organizational, and human-centered. Over time, this curiosity evolved into a structured approach to problem solving, combining research-driven thinking with practical implementation.

Academia & Research

I am currently a PhD scholar at Hamad Bin Khalifa University (HBKU), Qatar, where my work focuses on research-informed system design, data-driven analysis, and applied intelligence. My academic background includes peer-reviewed research publications and supervision of technical and research-based projects.

My research interests lie at the intersection of systems engineering, data analytics, intelligent systems, and real-world problem modeling.

Industry Experience

Alongside academia, I have gained hands-on industry experience in engineering and applied technology environments. This includes work in telecommunications and system laboratories, where I contributed to system testing, instrumentation, troubleshooting, and deployment.

This exposure allows me to bridge theory and practice — designing solutions that are not only technically sound but also deployable and scalable.

Leadership & Mentorship

I have held leadership and mentoring roles across academic and technical environments, including laboratory engineering positions and student-led initiatives. I have supervised and guided numerous student projects, supported curriculum development, and contributed to outcome-based education initiatives.

Awards & Recognition

My work and leadership have been recognized through multiple awards:

  • Leadership Award (2023) — HBKU, Qatar Foundation
  • Student Innovation Award (2023) — HBKU Innovation Office
  • Student Innovation & Entrepreneurship Technology Award — College of Science & Engineering, HBKU
  • HBKU Ideation Award (2025)

Innovation & Entrepreneurship

I am actively involved in innovation and venture-building initiatives. I am the founder of MotionID, a technology venture focused on developing intelligent, real-world solutions.

I have also been selected as an Incubatee at Qatar Science & Technology Park (QSTP), further strengthening my engagement with applied innovation, entrepreneurship, and industry collaboration.

Looking Ahead

My focus moving forward is on building impactful systems at the intersection of research, technology, and real-world needs — through IT solutions, innovation-driven projects, and collaborative ventures.
